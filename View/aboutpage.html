Study Material Management System


        A Study material management system (SMMS) is a software solution that helps department to handle course interactions for students. It creates an environment that can be accessed by teachers, students, and faculty members to update notes, broadcast notices, getting feedback or post questions and test. This system will reduce the wastage of time, and they will be able to easily get all the study information from their teachers. As a result, they will be able to continue their studies better.By using this application student will be able to check if their teacher has given them homework.And they can submit homework to their teacher through this online platform. Both the students and teacher will be benefited from these functionalities and it works to run more smoothly even though face-to-face meetings are not held very frequently.
